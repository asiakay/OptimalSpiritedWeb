PLEDGE & RULES

Participants Must Agree To Pay Themselves 25% Of Their Income - 15% To Save, And 10% To Invest.

Participants Must Not Buy Any One Thing That They Could Not Afford To Buy Five Of.

Participants Must Keep Track Of Their Spending For The First 30 Days To Establish A Budget.

Participants Must Set Up A Crypto Account on the platform of choice. We've tested Cash App, Coinbase and Webull.

The challenge is distributed via web platform. You control the simulation. This is not financial advice.
